## Recommended Action

Given RinaWarp's differentiation as a **terminal-first AI agent** (vs Cursor's IDE-first approach), I'd recommend:

1. **Increase Starter → $9.99** (matches Copilot, good entry point)
2. **Increase Pro → $19.99** (full feature, competitive with Tabnine)
3. **Add Lifetime at $249** (one-time, popular for developers)
4. **Keep a free tier** for viral growth

This positions RinaWarp:
- Cheaper than Cursor Pro ($20) 
- Competitive with Warp Individual ($15)
- Above Copilot ($10) - but offers more terminal-specific features
